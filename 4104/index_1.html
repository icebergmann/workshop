About Us

Dust Bowl Animal Rescue is a non-profit organization that works with foster homes primarily in the Midland/Odessa area as a no-kill rescue for animals in need. Our rescue efforts are geared towards not only dogs and cats, but also livestock as well including horses and other barnyard animals.

Donations

We rely on generous donations from the public in order to cover the costs of supplies to help animals in need. You can trust that your donation will be invested in a trustworthy organization since we are a Gold Participant in the GuideStar Exchange. GuideStar is a third party organization that rates public charities on their accountability and transparency to the public. With this, you can be certain that we will handle all of our donations in an honest and professional manner.
